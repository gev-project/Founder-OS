# Roadmap

> Long-term execution plan for building practical digital products through structured Product Development, Systems Thinking, and AI-Assisted Execution.

# Strategic Direction

## 2026 — Foundation Year

### Focus

**Build System → Build Product → Build Revenue**

Tahun 2026 adalah fase membangun fondasi.

Prioritas utama bukan membuat banyak produk.

Prioritas utama adalah membangun:

- Founder Operating System
- Product Development Workflow
- Documentation System
- Product Foundation
- First Revenue

### Primary Goals

- Membangun Founder OS yang lengkap
- Menyelesaikan fondasi SINOMAN™
- Meluncurkan MVP DigiMO
- Mendapatkan revenue pertama
- Membangun repositori dokumentasi yang terstruktur

---

## 2027 — Validation Year

### Focus

**Validate Products and Users**

Tujuan utama:

- Mendapatkan pengguna aktif
- Mengumpulkan feedback nyata
- Memvalidasi kebutuhan pasar
- Menyempurnakan Product Development Process

### Primary Goals

- Product-market validation
- User acquisition
- Product improvement
- Workflow optimization

---

## 2028 — Growth Year

### Focus

**Scale What Works**

Tujuan utama:

- Mengembangkan produk yang terbukti digunakan
- Mengoptimalkan monetisasi
- Mengembangkan product ecosystem

### Primary Goals

- Revenue growth
- Product expansion
- System scalability

---

## 2029–2030 — Ecosystem Year

### Focus

**Connected Product Ecosystem**

Tujuan utama:

- Integrasi antar produk
- Shared user experience
- Shared infrastructure
- Sustainable revenue

### Primary Goals

- Unified ecosystem
- Shared platform
- Long-term sustainability
